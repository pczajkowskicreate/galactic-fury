## BONUS TERRITORIES

Secret/automatic bonus encounters triggered between waves or after boss kills.
Each has a unique atmosphere and a rule the player must learn on the fly.

---

### Void Leech — *Leech Territory* [PLANNED / IN PROGRESS]
**Trigger:** Between Stage 2 boss death and Stage 3 start  
**Visual:** Vivid colorful gas clouds — pink, teal, orange — very different from the dark void of Stage 3  
**Mechanic:**  
- 12–14 Void Leeches spawn in a school-of-fish formation  
- They move as one unit, oscillating left-right in a shared sine wave  
- Every ~3s the whole school dives rapidly toward the player, then retreats to top  
- 3 dive cycles total; player can kill leeches during any phase  
- Killing the entire school early ends the bonus faster and gives better reward  
**Reward:** Health crystal + guaranteed shield drop  
**Ends when:** All leeches killed OR 3 dive cycles complete  

---

### Spore Moth — *The Nebula Trap*
**Trigger:** Bonus wave between waves 12 and 13 (Stage 3)  
**Visual:** Thick pink-purple nebula, reduced visibility (fog effect on camera), spore clouds already drifting  
**Mechanic:**  
- 4–5 Spore Moths hang stationary in different positions  
- Each moth continuously releases a drifting spore cloud that deals DoT on contact  
- Clouds overlap, creating maze-like danger zones  
- Player must navigate gaps to reach and destroy each moth  
- Clearing all moths "clears the air" — fog and clouds fade  
**Reward:** Weapon drop + score burst  

---

### Demon Hornet — *The Hive*
**Trigger:** Bonus wave between waves 16 and 17 (Stage 4)  
**Visual:** Massive organic hive structure on one side of the screen, yellow-orange pulsating glow  
**Mechanic:**  
- Hive spawns Hornet formations every 2–3s  
- The hive itself has HP — shooting it slows spawns, destroying it ends the bonus  
- Two strategies: fight the hornets (safe, smaller reward) or rush the hive (risky, big reward)  
**Reward:** Weapon combo + 2000 bonus score  

---

### Hellgate Herald — *The Rift*
**Trigger:** Bonus wave between waves 18 and 19 (Stage 4)  
**Visual:** Dimensional tear across the screen, hellish red/orange light from beyond  
**Mechanic:**  
- Herald floats in front of the Rift, keeping it open  
- Enemy types pour through the rift every few seconds  
- Two endings: kill the Herald quickly (closes rift, better reward) OR survive long enough for rift to collapse (smaller reward)  
- Decision must be made fast — surviving long is harder  
**Reward (fast):** All weapons + shield  
**Reward (survive):** Health crystal  

---

### Soul Devourer — *The Graveyard*
**Trigger:** Optional bonus between Stage 3 and Stage 4  
**Visual:** Dark void, pale blue glowing soul orbs floating (remains of destroyed ships)  
**Mechanic:**  
- Soul Devourers patrol between the orbs  
- Shooting a soul orb: gives big score burst BUT attracts nearby Devourers toward you  
- Safe play: kill Devourers only → smaller reward  
- Greedy play: shoot orbs while fighting → risk more, gain more  
**Reward:** Score multiplier boost + shield  

---

### Mantis Interceptor — *The Corridor*
**Trigger:** Bonus event mid-Stage 3 (after Void Cruiser defeat)  
**Visual:** Narrow gap between two massive void structures, red warning lights  
**Mechanic:**  
- Play field narrows to a vertical corridor for ~15 seconds  
- Mantis Interceptors make horizontal attack runs from alternating left/right sides, one at a time  
- Player can only move up/down — pure dodge and shoot  
- Each Interceptor that gets through without dying deals 1 damage  
**Reward:** Health crystal per interceptor killed  

---

### Scarab Bomber — *The Tomb Field*
**Trigger:** Bonus wave in Stage 2/3 transition  
**Visual:** Ancient alien structure in background, gold/brown Egyptian look, amber glow  
**Mechanic:**  
- Debris chunks float through the screen  
- Scarab Bombers cling to each chunk, dormant  
- Shooting a chunk destroys it — but releases the scarabs on it  
- A large "Queen chunk" at the end releases 6 scarabs at once  
- Player must decide: shoot chunks (remove cover) or navigate around them  
**Reward:** Multiple weapon drops  

---

### Spectrum Worm — *The Tunnel*
**Trigger:** Boss intro for a future Stage 5 or optional encounter  
**Visual:** The worm IS the environment — bioluminescent tunnel walls formed by its coiling body  
**Mechanic:**  
- Worm's body segments form shifting walls through the play area  
- Shoot segments to destroy them and open new paths (segments slowly regenerate)  
- Head snakes toward the player periodically to lunge — only head kill ends the encounter  
- Very cinematic, completely different pacing  
**Reward:** Unlock something (secret reward TBD)

## REGULAR ENEMIES (TO BE ADDED TO STAGES)

### Scarab Bomber — Stage 2/3 Regular
- HP: 4 | Enters from top, slow vertical descent
- **Attack:** Drops a slow-falling cluster bomb downward every 3.5s
- Bomb creates a small explosion radius on impact
- 15% weapon drop, 10% health crystal

### Spectrum Worm — Stage 3 Regular  
- HP: 5 | Sine-wave horizontal movement while descending
- **Attack:** Fires 3-spread shot toward player position every 4s
- Needs sustained fire to kill — functions as a tank
- 10% health crystal

### Spore Moth — Stage 3/4 Regular
- HP: 3 | Stationary hover, then slowly drifts
- **Attack:** Continuously releases small spore particles that drift downward (slow DoT cloud)
- Player must get close enough to kill it before the screen fills with spores
- 8% health crystal

### Mantis Interceptor — Stage 3/4 Elite
- HP: 6 | Enters fast from one side
- **Attack cycle:** Diagonal dash → fires 2 homing blades → retreats to top → repeats
- Single unit per wave, acts as a mini-boss encounter
- Guaranteed health crystal on kill

### Soul Devourer — Stage 4 Regular
- HP: 4 | Slow drift, ghostly
- **Attack:** Telegraph (tendrils extend, 0.5s warning) then fires slow tracking soul bolt
- On hit: drains player's score combo (not HP)
- 12% health crystal

### Demon Hornet — Stage 4 Swarm Replacement
- HP: 2 | Fast diagonal entry, targets player X
- **Attack:** Fires one sting bolt downward when near player Y, then exits screen
- Comes in groups of 3–4 like current swarms
- 5% health crystal

### Infernal Eye — Stage 4 Boss / Final Boss (future)
- The only creature not yet imported
- Vision: a massive floating eye with multiple phases
- Could be Stage 5 final boss or secret encounter
